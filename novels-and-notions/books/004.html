American Gods is a fantasy epic blending mythology and modern life. Shadow Moon, an ex-con, navigates a war between old gods and new in a sprawling, surreal America. Gaiman’s imaginative storytelling and rich characters make it a gripping read, adapted into a TV series. Published by William Morrow, an imprint of HarperCollins, it’s one of his most iconic works, showcasing his genre-defining creativity.

About Neil Gaiman

Neil Gaiman is a British author celebrated for his imaginative storytelling across genres. His novel The Graveyard Book won the Newbery Medal, and American Gods won the Hugo and Nebula Awards. Some of his works, such as Neverwhere, are published by Penguin Random House in certain editions. Gaiman's ability to blend myth, fantasy, and reality has earned him a dedicated global following.
